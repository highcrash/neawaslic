About

Small team. Long game.

Neawas is an independent software studio focused on self-hosted tools for the businesses that keep their neighborhood running — restaurants, shops, service providers, small distributors. We don't chase enterprise contracts. We ship products that pay for themselves in a month and keep working for years.

Every product we ship uses the same license server, same payment flow, and the same engineering principles. If you buy two of our products, you manage both from one place.

How we're different: we don't take a cut of your revenue (no SaaS fee), we don't rent you your data (it's on your server), and we don't end-of-life products (source is included — you can maintain a fork yourself if we go quiet).

Support model: email + Slack. One-time buyers get 12 months of updates and email support; recurring-tier customers (multi-tenant SaaS resellers) get priority and a direct channel.
